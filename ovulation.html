How to Calculate Your Fertile Window

Ovulation typically occurs about 14 days before your next expected period. For a 28-day cycle, that is around day 14. For a 32-day cycle, around day 18.

The fertile window spans approximately 6 days: the 5 days before ovulation plus ovulation day itself. This is because sperm can survive in the reproductive tract for up to 5 days.
