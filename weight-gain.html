Recommended Pregnancy Weight Gain

The Institute of Medicine (IOM) recommends different total weight gain ranges based on pre-pregnancy BMI. Most weight gain occurs in the second and third trimesters.

In the first trimester, expect 1–4 pounds total. After that, about 1 pound per week for normal weight, 0.5–0.7 lb/week for overweight. These are guidelines — discuss your specific situation with your provider.
