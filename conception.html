When Did Conception Occur?

Conception typically occurs around ovulation, which is about 14 days before the next expected period. From a due date, subtract 266 days to estimate conception.

This is an estimate. Sperm can survive up to 5 days, so conception could have occurred several days before or on the day of ovulation.
